The Land of Ancient Legends and Living Traditions

  • 🌍 About Batumi: Batumi is the second-largest city in Georgia and the capital of the Autonomous Republic of Adjara, located on the eastern coast of the Black Sea. It's one of the most popular travel destinations in the Caucasus region due to its unique blend of subtropical climate, cultural richness, and dynamic urban life.
  • 🏛️ History & Culture Ancient Roots: Batumi has ancient origins, dating back over 2,500 years. It was once a Greek colony known as "Bathus" (meaning deep harbor), and later a significant port under Roman and Ottoman rule.
